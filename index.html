how to play!

welcome to numbers in the box :)

  1. drag and drop pieces onto the board.

  2. your goal is to make the board read the same vertically and horizontally for each line. (or have a line of symmetry diagonally from top left to bottom right.)

    for example, this is a winning board:
    1 2 3
    2 4 5
    3 5 6

  3. good luck!
